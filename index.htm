POPULAR QUESTIONS
Our deep cleaning service includes:
  • Deep vacuuming
  • Organic shampooing
  • Stains, odor, pet hair removal
  • Bacteria and allergen reduction
  • Deep steam sanitizing to ensure a hygienic environment
We specialize in removing a wide range of stains including food and drink spills, pet stains, ink, dirt, and more.
Our advanced cleaning solutions and techniques effectively tackle even the toughest stains.
The duration of the cleaning process depends on the size of the item. For instance, cleaning a small couch usually takes about 1 hour, while a large sectional can take up to 2 hours.
Typically, it takes 8-10 hours for your couch to fully dry.
However, with our quick drying service using cold air fans, your furniture can be ready for use within 2-3 hours after cleaning.
We use eco-friendly and non-toxic cleaning products that are fully safe for your family and pets & ensure a healthy living environment.
